Academic Calendar (for TVET Students) 2019/2020 (2012 E.C)


First Term

 Nov 4, 2019 - Orientation for newly admitted students
 Nov 6-7, 2019 - Registration for Regular & Extension trainees
 Nov 11, 2019 - Class begins
 Nov 11-19, 2019 - Late Registration for Regular and Extension trainees
 Nov 19 - Dec 10, 2019 - LOs, UCs Assessment
 Dec 11 – Jan 9, 2020 - LOs, UCs Assessment
 Jan 10 – Feb 8, 2020 - LOs, UCs Assessment and UCs result submission to registrar
 Feb 17-21, 2020 - Last UCs result submission to registrar
 Feb 23, 2020 - First Term Class end

Second Term

 March 4, 2020 - Orientation for New entry Trainees
 March 5-6, 2020 - Registration for Regular & Extension trainees
 March 10, 2020 - Orientation for Trainers and administrative staffs
 March 12, 2020 - Second Term class begins
 March 14-20, 2020 - Late Registration for Regular and Extension trainees
 March 21-29, 2020 - LOs, UCs Assessment
 April 8 – May 7, 2020 - LOs, UCs Assessment
 May 8 – June 6, 2020 - LOs, UCs Assessment and UCs result submission to registrar
 June 7-21, 2020 - Last UCs result submission to registrar
 June 23, 2020 - Second Term Class end
 June 23 – July 7, 2020 - OS Institutional and COC Assessments for Level I – IV trainees will be given according the Scheduled of COC branch office

Third Term

 July 7, 2020 - Orientation for New entry Trainees
 July 8-9, 2020 - Registration for Regular & Extension trainees
 July 20, 2020 - Class begins
 July 25, 2020 - Graduation Day
 July 23-29, 2020 - Late Registration for Regular and Extension
 Aug 6 – Sep 4, 2020 - LOs, UCs Assessment
 Sept 10 – Oct 19, 2020 - LOs, UCs Assessment
 Oct 10 – Oct 19, 2020 - LOs, UCs Assessment and UCs result submission to registrar
 Oct 20-23, 2020 - OS Institutional and COC Assessments for Level I – IV trainees will be given according the Scheduled of COC branch office
 Oct 24, 2020 - Third Term Class end

Note:

• TVET Graduation day will be on the same date of Degree graduation program
• LO: learning Outcomes, UC: Unit competency, and OS: Occupational Standard
